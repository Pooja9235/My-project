Environment & Save Earth Initiatives
Topic Description Action
Plant Trees Trees absorb CO2 and provide oxygen. Organize a tree plantation drive.
Reduce Plastic Plastic harms marine life and pollutes land. Use cloth or jute bags instead.
Save Water Fresh water is limited and essential for life. Fix leaks,use buckets instead of showers.
Recycle Waste Recycling reduce pollution and consertial for life. Segregate dry and wet waste.
Use Public Transport Reduces carbon emission and traffic. Carpool or use buses /trains.
